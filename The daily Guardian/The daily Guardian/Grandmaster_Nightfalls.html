Grandmaster Nightfalls

The hardest content besides from raids and the Trials Of Osiris is the Grandmaster nightfall. Grandmaster Nightfall's cap the player at a certain level so the enemies will always hit harder than you do a single bullet or rocket from an enemy will wipe your team if you are not careful.
