What are Dungeons?

Dungeons are three player PVE endgame activities these have small based mechanics for each encounter think of them as a mini raid. But easier

The Shattered Throne

The Shattered Throne Dungeon was added back when the forsaken dlc was released this was also the first Dungeon to be added to the game.

Pit Of Heresy

The second Dungeon that was added to Destiny 2 was back when the Shadowkeep DlC was released The Pit of Heresy

Prophecy

The Third Dungeon that was added to Destiny 2 is called Prophecy

Grasp Of Avarice

The fourth Dungeon that was added to Destiny 2 was back when Bungie released the 30th anniversary pack to Destiny 2 This added a new Dungeon called The Grasp Of Avarice.
