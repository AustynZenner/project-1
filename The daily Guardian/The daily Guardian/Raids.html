What are raids?

Raids are the pinnacle of endgame content these are not matchmade activities which means you need to find 5 other players to go into these with these are heavily influenced by mechanics to complete these encounters but the loot from them is the best in the game.

When the Destiny 2 forsaken launched a new raid was released with it. The name of the raid is called Last wish.
